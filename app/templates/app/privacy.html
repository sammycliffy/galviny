Refunds Policy

Payouts
Investment profit can be withdrawn daily. The minimum that can be withdrawn per day is 1,000 Naira. Withdrawal is done once a day. Logistics fee of 200 Naira will be deducted for every withdrawal. Note that a waiting period of 10 days is required from the first day you lend us your money and also from the day of last withdrawal. However, if you wish to make withdrawal before your 10th day, a service charge of 2,000 Naira will be deducted and the balance paid to you.
