    How it works

    WITHDRAWAL

    Investment profit can be withdrawn daily. The minimum that can be withdrawn per day is 1,000 Naira. Withdrawal is done once a day. A commission of 200 Naira is deducted for every withdrawal made. This fee is used for logistics.

    RE-INVESTMENT

    Reinvestment is done after the duration of time stated for the trade group you invested in elapses. This means investment is done one at a time.

    REFERRAL

    This is to encourage participants to invite friends to invest with us. This is not mandatory. For every referral made, you get 3% of the FIRST CAPITAL your referral invested. This referral bonus will be added to the amount you can withdraw, so you can choose to withdraw it anytime you wish.
