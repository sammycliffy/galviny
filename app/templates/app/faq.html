F.A.Q



  1. What is Galviny all about

    2. Answer – Galviny is an investment platform with numerous trading communities that gives investors opportunity to partner with us. The businesses we invest in are carefully selected and are limited to business that gives daily profit which is shared daily with partners.

  2. Where does Galviny get money to pay investors?

    3. Answer – Galviny’s money are generated daily with very huge capital mainly from trading in FOREX, Crypto-currency and Oil and Gas business.

  3. Is Galviny a PONZI or HYIP?

    4. Answer – Galviny is neither a ponzi nor HYIP. Ponzi and HYIP have nothing they trade on. You rub Peter to pay Paul in Ponzi and HYIP, but in Galviny the money invested is used to trade and the profit is shared accordingly. Therefore, Galviny is not a Ponzi or HYIP.

  4. Is Galviny Registered with Cooperate Affairs commission?

    5. Answer – YES! Galviny is certified and registered with cooperate affairs commission

  5. How long does it take to get paid?

    6. Answer – Every profit made is shared daily with partners and every partner is entitled to withdraw profit and get credited. So once the profit is shared for the day, it can be withdrawn.

  7. Why the charge for each withdrawal?

    8. Answer – 200 Naira charge per withdrawal is used for logistics. Any amount whatsoever you are withdrawing have a fixed withdrawal charge of 200 Naira.
